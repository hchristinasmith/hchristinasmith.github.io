Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

I was at work, and we were in a group, with the CEO of the company trying to figure out new R&D pathways. One of the other interns took credit for one of my ideas. I valued harmony of the team or relationship, and instead of speaking up, I chose to keep quet to avoid political conflict. I weighed up integrity, the importance of being recognised for my contributions and ensuring fairness. And Harmony, perserving the team dynamic and not rocking the boat. Confronting the situation could have created discomfort, but staying silent felt like a compromise on my integrity. I chose to stay quiet in the moment, beasue i didnt want to cause fritction or complicate the situation. Next time, I might find a way to address similar issues with more confidence, perhaps in a way that balacens integrity and harmony more effectively. It might also help me consider how to approach situations like this in the future, knowing that finding a fair and respectful way to speak up is important - both for my own sense of integrity and for the strength of my relationships.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

While working in the lab, I asked a student who was helping me to take on a task that would allow me to be more productive and efficient. She declined, and I walked away feeling uneasy. Later, I apologised, thinking I may have been too pushy or controlling. Upon reflection, I realize I should have recognised the value of the work I was putting in and remained more confident in my leadership. I should have kindly but firmly communicated the importance of the task, as I was leading the work and it was essential for the task to be completed well and efficiently.
